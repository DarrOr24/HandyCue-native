# Delete Your HandyCue Account

To delete your HandyCue account, you must first log in to the HandyCue mobile app.

## Steps to delete your account
1. Open the HandyCue app
2. Log in to your account
3. Tap your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner
4. Open **Account**
5. Tap **Delete My Account**
6. Confirm the deletion

## Data that will be deleted
- Account profile
- Login credentials
- Saved user preferences and settings
- User-associated app data linked to the account

## Data that may be retained if required
- Limited technical logs
- Security or fraud prevention records
- Legal compliance records
