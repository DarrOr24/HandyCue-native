Delete Your HandyCue Account

You can delete your HandyCue account directly inside the HandyCue mobile app.

Steps

  1. Open HandyCue
  2. Go to Profile or Account Settings
  3. Tap "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Data deleted

  • Account profile
  • Login credentials
  • Saved user preferences/settings
  • User-associated app data linked to the account

Data that may be retained if required

  • Limited technical logs
  • Security / fraud prevention records
  • Legal compliance records
